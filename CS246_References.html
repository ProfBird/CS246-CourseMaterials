Books, Tutorials and Course Materials

Handbook of Software Engineering Methods—OER textbook includes chapters on Agile, UML, requirements, prototyping, and more.

The New Software Engineering—OER textbook by Sue Conger, 2008, Global Text Project.

Business Information Systems: Design an App for That—OER testbook.

  • Ch. 1: The information systems landscape: IS triangle, SDLC, transaction systems (ERP, SCM, CRM), collaboration systems, and business intelligence systems.
  • Ch. 2 and following: students plan, build, and develop a proposal for an iPhone application including a realistic mockup and a website to help market and support the app.
Course Materials Repository—Instructor's CS246 course materials on GitHub

LinkedIn Learning provides many high quiality ASP.NET MVC and other software development tutorials and courses. You can access LinkedIn Learning free of cost via the LCC Library home page. Click on the LinkedIn Learning icon near the bottom of the page for free LCC student access.
